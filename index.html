Our Tale

A few trails we have traversed over the past couple of years as a one dog and person pack. We've met many furry friends and had many encounters with wildlife as well as fantastic views. These are just a few of the areas we've explored and there are many more which are available if you are willing to search for them.

Walking Areas

Harold Parker State Forest State Forest located in the towns of Andover, North Andover, Middleton and North Reading.

With 35 miles of trails, 11 ponds, and dozens of campsites.

Salem Town Forest Town forest of Salem, New Hampshire.

This 347 acre forest is open daily from 30 minutes before sunrise to 30 minutes after sunset.

Winnekenni Park Algonquin for "very beautiful" located in the heart of Haverhill, MA.

Haverhill's largest park is one of the most popular and well known parks. Known for it's trails as well as Winnekenni Castle.
